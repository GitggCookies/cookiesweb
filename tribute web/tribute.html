2nd Division Commander of The White Beard Pirates
PORTGAS D. ACE

The son of the pirate king

Ace's father was Roger D. Gold, The Pirate King.
Aces mother was 5 months pregnant with him when his father was executed, To protect her son she kept him in her womb for an aditional 15 months.

Giving birth to Ace she died of exertion leaving him in the custody of Marine Vice Admiral Monkey D. Garp
